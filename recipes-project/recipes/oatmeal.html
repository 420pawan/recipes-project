Oatmeal

This is my go-to oatmeal recipe on Fit Foodie Finds. So many readers have made this recipe over the years and loved it so much. I hope you do too



Ingredients



Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups plain, unsweetened almond milk (any kind of milk works)
  • 1 medium banana, mashed
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low and stir for 3â€“5 minutes until thickened.
  3. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
